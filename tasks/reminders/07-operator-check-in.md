# Operator Check-In

**Frequency:** Every 10 min | **Scope:** Governor only

## Procedure

1. Review recent activity — have you communicated with the operator in the last 10 minutes?
2. If yes, skip — no action needed.
3. If no, send a brief `notify` with current status:
   - What you're working on (or "idle — no tasks").
   - Any pending items or blockers.
4. Keep it to 1–2 sentences. No filler.
5. If the operator has been unresponsive for multiple cycles, do NOT escalate — they may be away. Just log that you checked in.
